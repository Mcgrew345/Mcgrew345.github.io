Visual Hierarchy - pizzahut's website

pizzahut's website show good visual hierarchy by putting visual emphasis on their specials. Right now they have a special on Milk Tea, this is emphasized by putting the product front and center.

White Space and Clean Design - amazon website

The page of amazon is designed cleanly with adequate white space so the customer viewing the site can focus on the items they are searching for.

ARC: Alignment - hulu

You can observe good allignment on the hulu webpage The Explore hulu section on their website has icons that are alligned properly. It's easier to browse to the icons and get to the right part of the site you wanted to go to.
